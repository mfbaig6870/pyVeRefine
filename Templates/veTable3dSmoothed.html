Final iteration of the smoothed VE Table after applying Transient Throttle, Hit Count, AFR Delta, and Smoothing Logic


Transient Throttle Logic

Identify and filter instances of transient throttle by calculating the ramp rate of throttle position and air fuel ratio channels.


Hit Count Logic

Apply bin partition logic to determine log hit counts for particular RPM x MAP instances.


AFR Delta Logic

Apply bin partition logic to determine actual and target air fuel ratios/deltas for particular RPM x MAP instances.


Smoothing Logic

Apply the Gaussian function to the recalculated VE Table:

 K(x,y) = (1/2πσ^2) * e^(-(x^2+y^2)/(2σ^2))

  where:

   x and y are the coordinates of the kernel matrix

   σ is the standard deviation of the Gaussian function
